Sweet Jalapeño Cornbread

Description

This jalapeño cornbread recipe is a must-try for anyone who loves hot peppers and cornbread. A surprisingly tasty blend of flavors!

it has a great taste and makes for a great snack.

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup margarine, softened
  • ⅔ cup white sugar
  • 2 cups cornmeal
  • 1 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ⅔ cups milk
  • 1 cup chopped fresh jalapeno peppers, or to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. Beat margarine and sugar together in a large bowl until smooth.
  3. Combine cornmeal, flour, baking powder, and salt in another bowl. Take milk in a third bowl. Pour 1/3 of the milk and 1/3 of the flour mixture alternately into margarine mixture; whisk until just mixed. Repeat with remaining flour and milk mixtures; stir in jalapeño peppers. Spread mixture evenly into the prepared baking pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 22 to 26 minutes. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes before slicing.
